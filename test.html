About Fortnite

Fortnite is an online multiplayer game developed by Epic Games. Released in 2017, it has become one of the most popular battle royale games worldwide. Players fight to be the last one standing while building structures, collecting loot, and completing challenges.

Game Modes

  • Battle Royale: 100 players fight to survive on a shrinking map.
  • Zero Build: Classic Battle Royale without the building mechanics.
  • Creative Mode: Build your own worlds and play custom games.
  • Save the World: Co-op PvE mode with missions and zombie defense.
